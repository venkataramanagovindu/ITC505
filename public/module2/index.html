About

The Food Surplus App aims to tackle food waste by connecting surplus food from restaurants, cafes, and grocery stores with local charities and individuals in need. Our platform provides a simple and efficient solution to redistribute excess food and make a positive impact on both the environment and the community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce food waste and fight hunger by efficiently connecting food surplus with those in need. We aim to create a sustainable system that benefits both the environment and communities.

How It Works

Restaurants, cafes, and stores can easily list their surplus food on our platform. Charities and individuals in need can browse available listings and arrange pickups, ensuring no food goes to waste.

Our Impact

Since our launch, we've helped divert thousands of meals from landfills to those in need. Our network continues to grow, making a significant impact on reducing food waste and alleviating hunger.

Services

Our services include:

  • Connecting food donors with charities and individuals
  • Facilitating food pickup and delivery
  • Tracking and reporting food donations
  • Providing resources and support for food surplus management
